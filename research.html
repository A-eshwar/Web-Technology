Research Overview

The College has a well defined policy for research which covers research facilities and incentives. The college provides good infrastructure, high configuration computers and equipment to design and development of Research Projects. The College granted seed money and established EDC/NewGen Incubation Center, UAV Design Center, Centers for Advanced Antenna Design and Nano Technology Materials Research, with advanced software and equipment. College promotes innovation as evidenced by filing of numerous patents with financial support and incentives.

The College provides paid study leave for a period of 6 months three times during Ph.D., an allowance of Rs. 60,000 and an incentive of Rs. 25,000 at three stages of Ph.D. scholars. Digital Library/e-Resource access is provided to students and faculty members.

CVR Journal of Science & Technology was started in 2011 and publications in standard journals are encouraged. The Institution ensures implementation of code of ethics for research and more than 2000 research papers have been published in SCOPUS and Web of Science Journals. 22 professors have been identified as guides/supervisors. The College regularly organizes workshops/seminars on Research Methodology, IPR, Entrepreneurship and Skill Development.
